Race Ave Criterium

Race Ave Criterium was hosted in Lancaster PA, and happes annually. Nearly 70 riders competed in the category 4, and category 3-4 races, and it was a competetive and memorable day for the team. Team caprain Ray Delfing finished 1st in the category 4 race, and Zach Hojeibane and Aaron Rape both finished strong
