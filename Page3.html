Welcome to Watts & Whiskey

Lucarelli & Castaldi Branchbrook Spring Series classic, 2023

The Lucarelli & Castaldi Branchbrook Spring Series classic was the first race that the Watts and Whiskey team competed in in the 2023 season. On the cold March morning, Ray Delfing, Michael Zapantiz, Brad Kowalski, and Zach Hojeibane set off as part of the category 3-4 race. No podium positions were won, but it was a great chance for the team to get acclimated to the new season, and the commencement of race life.
